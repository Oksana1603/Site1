Is reading good for you?

There is a number of perks to reading.

  1. Entertainment
  2. Learning new things
  3. Broadening one`s vocabulary
  4. Ability to concentrate
  5. Relieving stress
  6. Practicing compassion

But how can you find time for reading in such a hectic time? The Reader will help you. Here are few ideas:

Read while commuting
Yes, we know. Sometimes the bus is crowded. Sometimes you just want to snooze. But sometimes it is not. Make your commute more fun with abook! And, if you are using e-book, you won`t have to worry about carrying a heavy tomme!
Read before you go to sleep
It`s not about a number of pages. It`s about a habit. Btw, it also helps you relax.
Start using audiobooks
Yes, that`s not for everyone, but give it a try. Try different books, so you can find a voice and intonation that work for you
Write notes
I hear you: you don`t really want to feel like back to school. But noone tells you to writes school type of notes. Write your own reflexions or use Mindmapping technique. Make them very concise. The process of summarising helps your brain store memories better. That makes reading more useful.
